🍲 About Us

Welcome to Sri Raghavendra Tiffens, your destination for authentic South Indian breakfast in Yelahanka, Bengaluru.

We serve fresh, hygienic, and delicious vegetarian tiffins prepared daily using traditional recipes and quality ingredients. Every dish is crafted with love and care to bring you the authentic taste of South Indian cuisine.

🍴 Our Menu

🍳 Idli

Soft, fluffy steamed cakes made from fermented rice and lentil batter. Served with sambar and chutneys. Light, nutritious, and perfect for breakfast.

🥘 Masala Dosa

Crispy golden crepe filled with spiced potato curry. A savory South Indian favorite made from fermented rice and lentil batter, served with sambar and chutneys.

🍤 Masala Vada

Crispy and spicy urad dal fritters with aromatic spices. Deep-fried to golden perfection with a crunchy exterior and savory inside. Perfect tea-time snack.

🍋 Lemon Rice

Tangy and aromatic rice dish with lemon juice, mustard seeds, and nuts. Light lunch option made with freshly cooked rice, peanuts, and cashews. Perfect for tiffin.

🍚 Pulav

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with vegetables and aromatic spices. A flavorful one-pot meal prepared in pressure cooker with traditional Indian cooking techniques.

🍅 Tomato Bath

Flavorful Karnataka-style tomato rice made with aromatic spices, coconut, and tempering. A quick one-pot meal perfect for lunch or dinner.

🍆 Vangi Bath

Brinjal (eggplant) rice with special spice powder and tamarind flavor. A popular Karnataka dish combining fresh brinjal with cooked rice and traditional masala.
